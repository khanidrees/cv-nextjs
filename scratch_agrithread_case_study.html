The Challenge

The agricultural marketplace is notoriously fragmented. We needed to build a unified hub that could seamlessly connect farmers, agronomists, B2B suppliers, and marketplace managers. The core challenge wasn't just data display—it was the synchronization of sensitive logistics across these four roles with zero margin for error or delay.

Technical Approach

To achieve true real-time synchronization, we bypassed traditional polling for a robust **Socket.io** integration. The backend was modularized into microservices to isolate the heavy transactional logic from the instant messaging and live-tracking streams.
